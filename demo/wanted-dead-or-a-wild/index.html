Wanted Dead or a Wild — What Is This Slot?

Wanted Dead or a Wild is a high-volatility Western-themed slot from Hacksaw Gaming, released on February 28, 2023. Built on a 5×4 grid with standard paylines, it departs from Hacksaw's cluster-pays experiments and delivers a more traditional reel structure — but with a twist that redefined what multiplier wilds can do.

The signature mechanic: Wild symbols carry individual multipliers from 1× to 100×. When multiple Wilds land on the same winning payline, their multipliers multiply each other — not add. Two 10× Wilds on the same line pay 100×, not 20×. Stack three mid-range Wilds and the math becomes explosive, which is what drives the 12,500× maximum win.

The Zug-Überfall (Train Robbery) Free Spins bonus, triggered by Sheriff's Badge Scatters, is where Wild Multipliers escalate progressively. Default RTP: 96.38%. One of Hacksaw Gaming's best-selling titles globally — ranking consistently in the top 5 most-played Hacksaw slots since launch.

Editorial note (slotshacksaw.com): Operator RTP can be configured below 89% — always verify the active RTP in the game's info panel before playing for real money.

How to Play Wanted Dead or a Wild

  1. Set your bet between €0.20 and €100 using the bet controls, then spin the 5×4 reel set.
  2. Form winning combinations on active paylines using standard left-to-right payline logic — at least 3 matching symbols starting from reel 1.
  3. Wild symbols substitute for all paying symbols and carry a multiplier value (1×–100×) displayed directly on the Wild.
  4. When a Wild contributes to a win, its multiplier applies to that win's payout. If multiple Wilds contribute to the same line win, their multipliers multiply together — two 5× Wilds on one line pay 25×, not 10×.
  5. Sheriff's Badge Scatter symbols (3 or more) trigger the Train Robbery Free Spins bonus. More Scatters = more initial free spins.
  6. Bonus Buy (where available): Purchase direct access to the Free Spins feature without waiting for organic scatter hits.
