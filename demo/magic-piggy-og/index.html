Magic Piggy OG — What Is This Slot?

Magic Piggy OG launched in May 2026 as one of Hacksaw Gaming's most mechanic-dense medium volatility releases. Set in a quirky farmyard world, the game is built around a system where Magic Piggy symbols transform via a Magic Hat that appears periodically on the reels. When the Hat lands, every active Piggy symbol on the grid converts simultaneously into one of three forms — and the outcome determines both your immediate win and how efficiently you accumulate Piggy Points toward the bonus round.

The slot runs on a standard payline format with RTP 96.20%, medium volatility, and a max win of 2,500× your stake. The relatively modest max win is offset by consistent base-game action — the Magic Hat fires regularly enough that sessions rarely feel completely dry, and the EPig Bonus (triggered by 4 scatters) features a non-resetting Piggy Point meter that creates a compounding accumulation loop unavailable in the standard Pig Bonus.

How to Play Magic Piggy OG

  1. Set your bet between the minimum and maximum stake, then spin the reels on a standard payline grid.
  2. Form winning combinations with standard symbol pays — Royal symbols (low) and themed high-pay symbols (Piggy-related characters).
  3. When a Magic Hat symbol lands, all active Magic Piggy symbols on the grid transform simultaneously. Each Piggy converts into: a Wild (substitutes all paying symbols), a Bill Stack (displays a cash value = face value × your active bet), or a Multiplier Coin (boosts the value of adjacent Bill Stacks).
  4. Piggy Point symbols land on the reels separately and contribute to your Piggy Point meter — the meter drives Hat activations in bonus rounds.
  5. Scatter symbols (FS icons) trigger the bonus: 3 scatters = Pig Bonus (10 FS), 4 scatters = EPig Bonus (10 FS with superior meter mechanics).

Magic Piggy OG Bonus Features Explained

Magic Hat Transformation
The core mechanic. Lands periodically on the reels and converts all active Piggy symbols in one of three ways: Wilds (substitute all paying symbols), Bill Stacks (face value × active bet — scales with your stake size), or Multiplier Coins (×2–×10 boost applied to adjacent Bill Stack values). Multiple simultaneous conversions compound rapidly.
Bill Stacks
Cash value symbols awarded when a Piggy converts via Hat. Bill Stack values are multiplied by your active wager — a €1 stake at a 5× Bill Stack pays €5, while a €10 stake pays €50. This stake-scaling is the primary driver of big wins and makes bet sizing consequential rather than proportional.
Multiplier Coins
Appear when a Piggy transforms to a Coin outcome. Each coin applies a multiplier (×2–×10) to all adjacent Bill Stack values on the grid. Landing multiple coins near multiple Bill Stacks creates a compound multiplier effect that drives the session high.
Piggy Point Meters
Separate meters that fill via Piggy Point symbols landing on the reels. In base play, meters reset after each Hat activation. In the EPig Bonus, meters do not reset — they carry over and compound across all 10 free spins, fundamentally changing the expected value of the bonus round.
