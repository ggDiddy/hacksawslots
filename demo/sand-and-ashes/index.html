Sand and Ashes — What Is This Slot?

Sand and Ashes launched in April 2026 as Hacksaw Gaming's most refined Egyptian-themed release to date. Built on a 5×5 grid, the game moves away from the cluster-pay format common to many grid slots and instead centres the entire win structure around Wild Scarab symbols — sticky wilds that carry their own multiplier value when they land. When multiple Scarabs occupy the grid simultaneously, their multipliers compound across every evaluated win, creating an exponential ceiling that rewards staying in a feature rather than chasing early exits.

The slot runs at RTP 96.27% with medium-high volatility and a max win of 10,000× your stake. Two special events — Firestorms and Sandstorms — interrupt base and bonus play to either add new multiplier-bearing wilds or reposition existing ones to wider grid coverage. At medium-high volatility, sessions are more streaky than a pure medium-vol title, but the Scarab system means big sessions are driven by mechanic logic rather than pure RNG luck.

How to Play Sand and Ashes

  1. Set your stake and spin the 5×5 grid. Wins form via adjacent symbol clusters or payline evaluations depending on the game's win logic.
  2. Watch for Wild Scarab symbols — when one lands, it sticks in place and displays its multiplier value (×2 to ×10+). It substitutes for all paying symbols on the grid.
  3. When multiple Scarabs are present, their multipliers stack. Two ×3 Scarabs covering a win path multiply that win by ×9. Three Scarabs compound further.
  4. A Firestorm event adds new wild positions to the grid and may upgrade existing Scarab multipliers — watch the grid flash before the event resolves.
  5. A Sandstorm reshuffles active Scarab positions. This can improve coverage dramatically or consolidate multipliers into a single high-value cluster.
  6. Land the required scatter count to trigger the free spins feature, where Scarabs accumulate across all spins without resetting — the primary route to max win territory.

Sand and Ashes Bonus Features Explained

Wild Scarab Multipliers
The central mechanic. Scarab symbols land on the 5×5 grid with an attached multiplier (×2–×10+) and remain sticky for the duration of the feature. They substitute for all paying symbols. When multiple Scarabs contribute to the same win, their multipliers compound — two ×5 Scarabs on a win path deliver a ×25 total multiplier to that win.
Firestorm
A random event that activates during base play and free spins. When triggered, the Firestorm adds new wild positions to the grid and can increase the multiplier values of existing Scarabs already in place. Back-to-back Firestorms during a free spins run are the primary driver of the 10,000× ceiling.
Sandstorm
A grid-reshuffling event that repositions all active Wild Scarabs to new grid locations. Outcomes vary — a Sandstorm can spread Scarabs to cover more win paths simultaneously, or consolidate them into a cluster near high-value symbol positions. Unpredictable but always consequential when Scarabs are already on the grid.
Free Spins Feature
Triggered by landing the required scatter count. During free spins, Wild Scarabs persist across all spins rather than resetting each turn. Each new Scarab that lands adds to the existing collection. Firestorm and Sandstorm events continue to fire, compounding the Scarab multiplier stack across the full free spins run.
