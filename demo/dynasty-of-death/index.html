Dynasty of Death — What Is This Slot?

Dynasty of Death is a Hacksaw Gaming high volatility payline slot set in a dark undead empire. The game is built around a wild multiplier system where Wild symbols carry multiplier values (2×–10×) and compound together when multiple Wild Multipliers appear on the same payline. A 3× and a 5× wild on the same line = a 15× multiplier on the resulting win.

Running at RTP 96.25% with high volatility and a 10,000× max win, this is a Hacksaw title built for players comfortable with dry base game sessions in exchange for explosive bonus potential. The free spins feature — triggered by 3+ scatters — amplifies the compounding multiplier system with persistent wild multipliers that carry across all bonus spins without resetting.

How to Play Dynasty of Death

  1. Set your bet and spin on the standard payline grid. Standard symbol pays apply across active paylines.
  2. Wild Multiplier symbols land on the reels and act as both wilds (substituting for all paying symbols) and multipliers (applying their value to any win they complete). Values range from 2× to 10×.
  3. When multiple Wild Multipliers appear on the same payline, their values compound — they multiply together rather than add. Two 5× wilds on the same line = 25× total multiplier.
  4. Land 3 or more scatter symbols to trigger the free spins bonus: 3 scatters = 10 FS, 4+ scatters = 15 FS.
  5. In the free spins bonus, wild multipliers are persistent — they do not reset between spins. Wilds that land accumulate their multiplier values across the full bonus round.

Dynasty of Death Bonus Features Explained

Wild Multiplier Symbols
The core mechanic. Wild Multipliers land on the reels and substitute for all paying symbols while applying their multiplier (2×–10×) to any win they complete. The range of values creates frequent small multiplier wins and rarer high-multiplier explosions. In the base game, each spin is independent — multipliers only compound within a single spin.
Compounding Multiplier Stacks
When two or more Wild Multipliers appear on the same paying line, their values multiply together: a 3× and a 4× = 12×; a 5× and a 10× = 50×. Three compounding wilds can reach 1000× on a single payline. This stacking mechanism is the primary driver of massive wins and represents the highest single-spin win potential in the game.
Free Spins Bonus
Triggered by 3+ scatter symbols. Awards 10–15 free spins depending on scatter count. The critical difference from the base game: wild multipliers are persistent across all free spins. Each wild that lands in the bonus adds its multiplier to a persistent stack that applies to all subsequent wins. By the end of the bonus, the accumulated multiplier stack can reach extreme values.
Scatter Symbols
Scatter symbols land on all reels and pay regardless of payline position. 3 scatters trigger the bonus; 4+ scatters award the maximum 15 free spins. Additional scatter symbols landing during the free spins round can retrigger additional spins, extending the bonus window and giving more opportunity for multiplier accumulation.

RTP & Variance

Default RTP: 96.25%. Volatility: High. Dynasty of Death sits in the high volatility band — expect extended dry spells in the base game before bonus triggers. The payoff when the bonus fires with a built-up multiplier stack can be substantial, but patience and adequate bankroll are prerequisites.
