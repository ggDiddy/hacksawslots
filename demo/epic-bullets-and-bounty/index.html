Epic Bullets & Bounty — What Is This Slot?

Epic Bullets & Bounty is a Hacksaw Gaming slot set in a gritty wild west world where you play as a bounty hunter. The game is built around a bullet chamber mechanic — bullet symbols accumulate in a displayed chamber during base play, and the loaded chamber carries into the free spins bonus where each bullet fires and applies its multiplier value to wins during that spin.

Running at very high volatility with a 15,000× ceiling and RTP 96.35%, this is one of the most demanding titles in the Hacksaw catalogue in terms of bankroll requirements. The base game can run for extended periods without significant wins — this is by design. The bullet chamber mechanic rewards patience: a fully loaded chamber entering the free spins bonus can generate explosive sequential multiplied wins across the entire bonus round.

How to Play Epic Bullets & Bounty

  1. Set your bet and spin. A bullet chamber display is visible at the top of the screen showing loaded bullets and their multiplier values.
  2. Bullet symbols land on the reels during the base game and are loaded into the chamber automatically. Each bullet carries a multiplier value assigned at the time of loading.
  3. Separately, bounty symbols fill a bounty meter during base play — when the meter fills, a bounty reward is collected.
  4. Land scatter symbols to trigger the free spins bonus. The bullet chamber carries over from the base game — bullets you loaded in the base game are available to fire in the bonus.
  5. During free spins, bullets fire on specific spins applying their multiplier to all wins on that spin. Persistent chambers across free spins allow multiple bullet firings within a single bonus round.

Epic Bullets & Bounty Bonus Features Explained

Bullet Chamber Mechanic
The core mechanic. Bullet symbols land during the base game and load into a visible chamber. Each bullet is assigned a multiplier value at the time it loads. The chamber is persistent — bullets loaded in the base game carry directly into the free spins bonus without being spent. A fully loaded chamber entering the bonus significantly elevates total bonus expected value.
Bullet Firing in Free Spins
Once free spins trigger, loaded bullets fire during the bonus round. Each time a bullet fires, its multiplier value is applied to all wins produced on that free spin. With multiple bullets loaded and firing across a 10+ free spin sequence, individual free spins can reach very high multiplied win totals — the primary route to the 15,000× max win.
Bounty Collection
A secondary mechanic operating in the base game. Bounty symbols land on the reels and fill a bounty progress meter. When the meter is full, a bounty reward is awarded — typically cash prizes or enhanced bullet loads. Bounty collection provides win activity during the base game dry periods typical of very high volatility titles.
Free Spins Bonus
Triggered by landing scatter symbols. Free spins carry all bullets loaded in the base game and continue to load new bullets during the bonus round itself. The combination of base-game and bonus-loaded bullets can create sequences where multiple consecutive free spins have bullets firing — generating sustained multiplied win output throughout the bonus.
